Child Safety Standards for Yod

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Our Commitment

Yod has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and actively work to prevent, detect, and respond to any child safety concerns.

1. Age Restrictions and Verification

  • Minimum Age: Users must be 18 years or older to create an account
  • Age Verification: Users provide their date of birth during registration
  • Account Monitoring: We actively monitor for accounts that may belong to minors
  • Immediate Action: Any account found to belong to a user under 18 is immediately terminated

2. Content Moderation and Safety Measures

  • Image Screening: All uploaded images are reviewed for inappropriate content
  • User Reports: In-app reporting system allows users to flag concerning content or behavior
  • Rapid Response: All child safety reports are prioritized and reviewed immediately
  • Account Suspension: Accounts violating child safety policies are immediately suspended pending investigation
  • Content Removal: Any content suspected of involving minors is immediately removed

3. Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

Yod implements multiple layers of protection against CSAM:

  • Proactive Detection: We employ automated systems and manual review to detect potential CSAM
  • Hash Matching: Images are checked against known CSAM databases where legally required
  • User Education: Community guidelines clearly prohibit any content involving minors
  • Permanent Bans: Users found sharing CSAM are permanently banned
  • Data Preservation: Evidence is preserved for law enforcement investigations

4. Reporting to Authorities

Yod complies with all applicable laws regarding reporting of child exploitation:

  • NCMEC Reports: We report suspected CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) as required by US law
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • International Compliance: We comply with reporting requirements in all jurisdictions where we operate
  • Timely Reporting: Reports are submitted promptly upon discovery of suspected violations

5. User Reporting System

Users can report child safety concerns through multiple channels:

  • In-App Reporting: Flag button available on all user-generated content
  • Report Categories: Specific option for reporting child safety concerns
  • Anonymous Reporting: Users can report concerns without identifying themselves
  • 24/7 Availability: Reporting system is available at all times

6. Staff Training and Protocols

  • All team members receive training on identifying and responding to child safety concerns
  • Clear escalation procedures ensure rapid response to suspected violations
  • Regular updates to policies and procedures based on emerging threats and best practices
  • Designated child safety officer oversees compliance and response procedures

7. Privacy and Data Protection for Minors

  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18
  • If we discover an account belongs to a minor, all associated data is immediately deleted
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations worldwide

8. Community Guidelines

Our Community Guidelines explicitly prohibit:

  • Any content depicting, encouraging, or promoting the sexual exploitation of children
  • Attempting to establish inappropriate contact with minors
  • Grooming behavior or solicitation of minors
  • Sharing or requesting CSAM
  • Any content that sexualizes minors in any way

9. Ongoing Improvements

  • Regular security audits and safety assessments
  • Continuous monitoring of emerging threats and trends
  • Investment in advanced detection technologies
  • Collaboration with child safety organizations and experts
  • Regular policy updates to address new challenges

Emergency Reporting

If you encounter content that depicts child sexual abuse or exploitation:

  • Report it immediately through the in-app reporting system
  • Email us at: safety@mrcto.ai
  • Report directly to NCMEC: CyberTipline
  • Contact local law enforcement if you believe a child is in immediate danger

10. Compliance and Accountability

  • We maintain detailed records of our child safety efforts and incident responses
  • We comply with all regional and national child safety laws
  • We respond to lawful requests from authorities within required timeframes
  • We publish transparency reports on our safety enforcement actions
